Jon Roobottom's CV (Résumé)

I create digital products and services

I'm a designer with 20 years of experience in creating digital products and websites.

I was born and bred in the industrial Black Country I always wanted to follow my dad and grandfather into a career in engineering. I studied electrical engineering at University but a year in I rediscovered my love of design and switched my degree.

Now living by the sea in Sussex, I have a foot in both worlds: I bring a no-nonsense approach to the design process; a ton of practical, hands-on expertise in building things for the web; and systems-thinking in everything I do.

Great work always comes from working with others, and over the last seven years, I've led teams of designers and developers in creating things that people love.
