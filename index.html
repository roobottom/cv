Introduction

I create digital products and services

I'm a designer with 20 years of experience in creating digital products and websites.

Born and bred in the industrial Black Country — I always wanted to follow my father and grandfather into a career in engineering. I studied electrical engineering at University, but a year in I rediscovered my love of design and switched my degree.

Now living by the sea in Sussex, I have a foot in both worlds: I bring a no-nonsense approach to the design process; a ton of practical, hands-on expertise in building things for the web; and systems-thinking in everything I do.

Great work always comes from working with others, and over the last seven years, I've led teams of designers and developers in creating things that people love.

From April 2017: Head of Design for product and communications at Firefly Learning

Organisation
Firefly Learning
Position
Head of Design
Location
London, United Kingdom

As Head of Design I was part of Firefly's senior leadership team. I was in charge of Firefly's design efforts across product and brand communications. I led key initiatives that helped cement Firefly's reputation as a leader within the EdTech space:

  • I led the creation of a highly complex pattern library. Consisting of over 500 reusable components, it detailed specifications for developers.
  • I shaped a research and design process that enabled greater creative thinking from others within the business, not just designers.
  • I played a key role in a reorganisation of the design, product and development teams which saw a huge improvement in output and quality levels.
  • I led my team in the research and design of features which helped unlock new markets.
  • I directed a re-brand effort which included redefining our purpose, beliefs and values helping to align the company under a reinvigorated shared mission.

From September 2014: Head of design and front-end development at Firefly Learning

Dates
From September 2014 Until April 2017
Organisation
Firefly Learning
Position
Head of Design
Location
London, United Kingdom

I ran a combined design and front-end development team as part of Firefly's senior leadership team and oversaw some key initiatives that helped build the foundation for a robust, future-proof web application:

  • I designed and hand-coded several new features in HTML, CSS & React
  • I played a pivotal role in the creation of a single source-of-truth front-end pattern library (React, Node.js and Nunjucks) that enabled developers to reference live components rather than using static copies.
  • I co-created a smart recolouring system that allowed us to quickly apply school brands to our existing templates.
  • In November 2016 the leadership team raised £4.5 million — the largest ever series A for a UK EdTech company — from BGF Ventures and Beringia. Usability and design were a key part of their decision to invest.
  • I oversaw splitting out design and front-end to enable us to rearchitect our front-end in React and align our entire technology stack.
